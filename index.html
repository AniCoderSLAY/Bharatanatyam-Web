Welcome to Bharatanatyam Web!!

Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest classical dances of India. It is a dance form of the Indian subcontinent, particularly of the state of Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Bharatanatyam is a classical dance form that is intricately choreographed and has a strict code of ethics and discipline. It is a spiritual expression of the devotee and is often performed in front of the Lord, thus the dance is considered to be a form of worship.

Bharatanatyam Web is a website that will help you learn how to excel Bharatanatyam. It is a fun dance but also serious. It is a way of life and the dance is not just a form of entertainment but a form of self-expression and an offering to the divine. The dance requires skill, discipline, and dedication and is a great way to learn discipline, self-expression, and self-discipline.
